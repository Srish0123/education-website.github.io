we have best courses for you

Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology [MIET] is an engineering and management college offering 28 courses in total with 6 different streams. Undergraduate programs offered by the institute are B.Tech., B.Pharm., and B.Voc. Postgraduate programs offered by the college are M.Tech, MCA, M.Pharm, and MBA. Admission to all the courses is entrance-based except for the B.Voc program, admission to which is based on merits obtained by the student in the previous qualifying exam. Graduates from the college are placed in top-recruiting companies such as Accenture, Genpact, Axis Bank, L & T, etc.
